A Legacy of Care

For 50 years, Valley-Wide Health Systems has been the heart, providing high-quality, accessible, and affordable healthcare to our communities across the San Luis Valley and Southern Colorado. We have grown from a small clinic into a comprehensive healthcare home.

This gala is our Emerald City—a destination of hope and future possibilities. Funds raised will support the establishment of the Valley-Wide Foundation catalyzing future development across our medical, behavioral, dental, and physical therapy service lines.

"It is with immense pride that I invite you to join us. We look forward to celebrating with you."

Jania Arnoldi

President/CEO
