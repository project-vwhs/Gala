A Legacy of Care. A Foundation for the Future.

For 50 years, Valley-Wide Health Systems has been steadfast in its mission to provide high-quality, accessible, and affordable healthcare to our communities across the San Luis Valley and Southern Colorado. We have grown from a small clinic into a comprehensive healthcare home for thousands, and this achievement was only possible through the enduring support of partners like you.

This gala is more than just a celebration; it is a critical fundraiser to ensure our legacy of care continues for the next 50 years. The funds raised will support the establishment of the Valley-Wide Foundation, which will catalyze future development across our medical, behavioral, dental, and physical therapy service lines.

"It is with immense pride and gratitude that I invite you to join us in celebrating this incredible milestone. We look forward to celebrating with you."

Jania Arnoldi

President/CEO, Valley-Wide Health Systems
